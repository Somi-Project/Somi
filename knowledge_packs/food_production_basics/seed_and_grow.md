# Seed and Grow Basics

Recovery agriculture starts with reliable water, protected seed stock, and a
small repeatable growing area.

Priorities:

- save and label seed by date, source, and germination notes
- start small with crops that match local climate and calorie needs
- protect soil with mulch, compost, and runoff control
- separate healthy composting from waste streams that can spread disease

When scaling up:

- keep a crop rotation log
- test simple protected systems such as shade cloth, raised beds, or
  aquaponics only after the water loop is stable
