# Battery and Microgrid Basics

Restore the smallest stable power loop first, then add loads carefully.

Checklist:

- inspect cables, fuses, grounds, and battery temperature before energizing
- bring up essential loads first: lighting, radios, refrigeration, water
  treatment, and medical devices
- log voltage, charge state, and load changes instead of guessing
- isolate faulty branches before reconnecting the wider shelter

Good habits:

- keep spare fuses dry and labeled
- avoid deep discharge when you can rotate loads or schedule charging windows
- document safe shutdown and restart order for inverters and charge controllers
